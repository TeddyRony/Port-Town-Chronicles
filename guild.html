The Guild

The men and women who operate under Tedronai's banner. Full profiles updated as characters are documented.

Guild Member

Stilgar

Human Myrmidon · Level 8 · Neutral Good

6'3", 230 lbs. Black hair, blue eyes. Field commander and record-keeper for Tedronai's operations in Port Town. Longest-serving operative. Reluctant leader by appointment — equally comfortable as just another blade on a team. Manages Tedronai's holdings when not in the field.

Weapons: Two-handed sword (master), longsword (proficient)
Armor: Full plate
Items: Ring of Jumping, Ring of Feather Fall

Proficiencies: Reading/Writing, Rope Use, Land-Based Riding, Iron Will, Endurance, Leadership, Fire Building, Ancient History

Guild Member

Juniper

5'5", 112 lbs. Red hair, green eyes. Forest druid and wanderer. Worships Ehlonna. Carries a quarterstaff — specialized. Has never used her shapechange ability in combat yet.

Weapons: Quarterstaff +3 (specialized), Dagger +1, Sling
Armor: Leather Armor +3
Items: Ring of Protection +2, Ring of Shooting Stars

Languages: Common, Druidic, Elvish, Sylvan, Treant, Goblin, Pixie, Dryad, Centaur

Guild Member

Raythe

Elf Ranger · Level 8 · Neutral Good

5'8", 140 lbs. Black hair, green eyes. One of the guild's founding members and the party's original scout in the early adventures. Has a particular and well-documented hatred of Ogres. Fights with two swords simultaneously, both wielded with expert proficiency.

Weapons: Longsword Firebrand (expert), Short Sword (expert)
Items: Onyx Dog Figurine (summons Fenris, war dog)

Guild Employer

Tedronai

Human Wild Mage · Level 13 · Chaotic Good

6'0", 185 lbs. Brown hair, green eyes. Still young. Employer of the guild and the mind behind its operations in Port Town. Rarely engages in direct combat. DEX 16 surprises those who expect a bookish wizard; INT 21 surprises everyone else. A Wild Mage who keeps that fact close to his chest. Owns a galleon and employs a scribe named Jarvis.

Weapons: Quarterstaff, Dagger of Throwing
Items: Cloak of the Manta Ray, Slippers of Spider Climbing, Belt of Many Pouches, Ring of Regeneration, Ring of Protection

Guild Associate

Dr. Jill Jensil

Human Alchemist · Biogenesis · Resident Guild Alchemist

5'9", 110 lbs, 27 years old. Red hair, blue-green eyes. Encountered mid-experiment — whatever she was working on likely caused her transformation into Mrs. Hilde. Recruited by Tedronai on the spot.

Dr. Jill: STR 10, DEX 9, CON 12, INT 17, WIS 13, CHA 9
Mrs. Hilde: STR 19, DEX 17, CON 18, INT 4, WIS 9, CHA 4

Proficiencies: Alchemy, Anatomy, Glassblowing, Reading/Writing, Brewing, Pottery, Herbalism, Agriculture, Cooking

Retired PC

Lord Klaude

Lord of Port Town · Played by Colton

A long-running character now retired from active play — his level and standing too significant to run alongside the current party. Now serves as the lord of Port Town and the man behind the current mission, having provided Tedronai with the gem of true teleportation to retrieve the relic needed to end the siege.
