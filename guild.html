Port Town · Operating under Tedronai's Banner

The Guild

Those who answer when the city calls. A record of the men and women who operate under the guild's banner — their roles, their histories, and their standing.

Active Members

Guild Member Stilgar Human Myrmidon · Neutral Good

Field commander and record-keeper for Tedronai's operations. The guild's longest-serving operative and a reluctant leader by appointment — just as comfortable as one blade among many. Manages Tedronai's holdings in Port Town when not in the field. Has seen enough to be surprised by very little.

Guild Member Juniper Wood Elf Druid · Neutral Good

A forest druid and wanderer who follows Ehlonna. Quiet in the way that deep forests are quiet — not empty, just watching. Draws on the natural world for both counsel and power, and speaks languages most of the party has never heard. Her shapechange ability remains largely untested in the field.

Guild Member Rancor Barbarian

A barbarian who respects strength above ceremony. One of the party's heavies — a natural partner to Stilgar in the vanguard. Has a warrior's economy of words and a fighter's instinct for where the real danger is. Details to be added.

Guild Member Pyril Cleric

The party's cleric and spiritual anchor. Steady under pressure, which is fortunate given the number of times the party has needed emergency intervention. Has a tendency to slide down smooth ramps into Stilgar. Details to be added.

Guild Member Keyo Monk

A monk who fights without weapons and makes it look unremarkable. Demonstrated in the pyramid that shadow creatures will not attack what they do not perceive as a threat — a lesson that may yet prove useful. Calm in situations that unsettle everyone else. Details to be added.

Guild Member Diana Ranger

A ranger and regular member of the party. Missed the pyramid expedition after a character sheet went missing — replaced on short notice by a wizard who shall not be forgotten. Details to be added.

Guild Member Raythe Elf Ranger · Neutral Good

One of the guild's founding members and its original scout. An elven ranger who moves through the wilds like he belongs there — because he does. Fights with two blades simultaneously and holds a fierce, personal hatred of Ogres. Accompanied by Fenris, a war dog who has earned his place at the party's side.

Leadership

Guild Employer Tedronai Human Mage · Chaotic Good

The mind and money behind the guild's operations in Port Town. Methodical, observant, and considerably more capable in a fight than he appears. Prefers to direct from the rear and intervene with precision when needed. Owns a galleon and employs a personal scribe. His true nature is known to few.

Lord of Port Town Lord Klaude Notable Figure

Lord of Port Town and a figure of formidable power and long history. It was Klaude who tasked Tedronai with retrieving the relic needed to end the siege, providing the gem of true teleportation to make the mission possible. His full history is its own chronicle.

Guild Associates

Guild Associate Dr. Jill Jensil Alchemist · Biogenesis

Resident alchemist and the guild's most unusual recruit. Encountered mid-experiment and brought into the fold by Tedronai, who recognized her value immediately. Brilliant within her field and self-sufficient in ways that go well beyond the laboratory. Known by some members to have an alternate form. The matter is treated with discretion.

Provisional

Provisional Slip Rogue · Contracted

A contracted thief who is in it for the coin — her words, not a criticism. Currently under evaluation. Demonstrated real resourcefulness in the pyramid, including pulling a stunned Pyril clear of danger at personal cost. Whether she stays will depend on what comes next.

Provisional The Wizard Wizard · Stand-in

Rolled up on short notice when a character sheet went missing. Opened the pyramid's first encounter by attempting to greet winged demon-serpents in what he believed was Abyssal. Results were instructive for everyone present. Full name pending. Future status uncertain.
