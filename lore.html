The World · Port Town and Beyond

Lore & Wiki

A growing record of locations, factions, notable figures, and the history of the campaign world. Updated as the party learns more.

Location

Port Town

A port city currently under siege. Home to the guild and the seat of Lord Klaude's authority. Population and political details to be added.

Location

The Black Crystal Pyramid

A volcanic structure of obsidian-like crystal. Purpose and origin unknown. Contains at least one piece of the relic. Guarded by ash-creatures, shadow beings, and pillar-controlling entities. The party is currently inside.

Location

The Wizard's Lair

An underground wizard's lair claimed by Tedronai. Filled with books and magical resources. Currently guarded by the pixies of the nearby forest in exchange for past services rendered. Has not been visited in some time.

Location

The Pixie Forest

A forest inhabited by pixies led by Mountain Dew. The guild stumbled in during another mission, were knocked unconscious, and woke to a job offer. After rescuing Mountain Dew's wife Ruby Red, the pixies became allies — guarding the wizard's lair and watching over the guild's thunderhawks while the party adventures.

Location

Blankenshire

A township to the east, once seat of Jahayne Cote's evil empire. The party investigated and found normal peasants living under a drab, oppressive rule — soulless and defeated. The town served as the base of operations for one of the guild's main villains across several campaigns.

Item

The Gem of True Teleportation

Provided by Lord Klaude to Tedronai. Three uses, three locations, three relic pieces. Two uses remain.

Item

The Eternal Kindling

Sticks recovered from the pyramid's first level. Flame burns real but the wood does not char. Significance unknown. Most party members carry a piece.

Asset

The Thunderhawks

Gifted to the guild by Mountain Dew following the rescue of Ruby Red. Currently kept and watched over by the pixies while the party adventures. Details on number and individual hawks to be added.

Asset

Tedronai's Galleon

A galleon owned by Tedronai. Details on crew, name, and home port to be added. Represents significant logistical capability for the guild.

Retired PC

Lord Klaude

Lord of Port Town and a figure of considerable power and history. A long-played character now retired from active adventuring — his level simply too high to run alongside the current party. Commissioned Tedronai to retrieve a relic capable of ending the siege, providing the gem of true teleportation to make it possible.

NPC

Tedronai

Wild Mage and guild employer. Operates from Port Town, managing both the guild and various holdings in the region. Intelligent, measured, and less helpless in a fight than he appears. His true nature as a Wild Mage is not widely known.

NPC

Jarvis

Tedronai's personal scribe. Neutral Good, INT 13. Manages records and correspondence for Tedronai's operations. Reliable and capable — more than a note-taker.

Guild Associate

Dr. Jill Jensil

Resident alchemist specializing in biogenesis. The guild encountered her mid-experiment — whatever she was working on likely caused her transformation into Mrs. Hilde, a powerful alter ego known to select guild members. Recruited by Tedronai on the spot. Her research continues under guild protection.

Ally

Mountain Dew

Leader of the pixie forest. Ambushed the guild on first contact, then enlisted their help to rescue his wife Ruby Red. Rewarded the party with thunderhawks and an ongoing alliance. Currently watches over the guild's mounts and the wizard's lair.

Ally

Ruby Red

Wife of Mountain Dew, the pixie forest leader. Was taken by unknown parties and rescued by the guild. Her rescue cemented the alliance between the party and the pixies.

Guild Member

Raythe

Elven ranger and one of the guild's founding members. Served as the party's primary scout in the early adventures. Expert dual-swordsman with a firebrand longsword and a well-known hatred of Ogres. Travels with Fenris, a war dog summoned via an Onyx Dog figurine.

Villain

Jahayne Cote

Leader of an evil empire based in Blankenshire to the east. The party investigated his territory and found an oppressed, soulless population under his rule. Became the main villain across several campaigns. Responsible for unleashing a plague on Port Town. Eventually escaped after the party bested his forces and left Trex Bullock for dead.

Former Villain / Ally

Trex Bullock

Half-ogre general and feared lieutenant of Jahayne Cote. A major antagonist across several adventures — significantly higher level than the party for much of that time. After the guild's eventual showdown with Jahayne's forces, Trex was left for dead by his own master. He survived and later became head of a silver dragon's guard, finding a new and unlikely purpose after his betrayal.
