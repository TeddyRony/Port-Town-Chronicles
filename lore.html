The World · Freeport and Beyond

Lore & Wiki

A growing record of locations, factions, notable figures, and the history of the campaign world. Updated as the party learns more.

Location

Freeport

A port city currently under siege. Home to the guild and the seat of Lord Klaude's authority. Population and political details to be added.

Location

The Black Crystal Pyramid

A volcanic structure of obsidian-like crystal. Purpose and origin unknown. Contains at least one piece of the relic. Guarded by ash-creatures and pillar-controlling entities.

Item

The Gem of True Teleportation

Provided by Lord Klaude to Tedronai. Three uses, three locations, three relic pieces. Two uses remain.

Item

The Eternal Kindling

Sticks recovered from the pyramid's first level. Flame burns real but the wood does not char. Significance unknown.

Retired PC

Lord Klaude

Lord of Freeport and a figure of considerable power and history. A long-played character now retired from active adventuring — his level simply too high to run alongside the current party. Commissioned Tedronai to retrieve a relic capable of ending the siege, providing the gem of true teleportation to make it possible.

Guild Associate

Dr. Jill Jensil

Resident alchemist specializing in biogenesis. The guild encountered her mid-experiment — whatever she was working on likely caused her transformation into Mrs. Hilde, a powerful alter ego known to select guild members. Recruited by Tedronai on the spot. Her research continues under guild protection.

NPC

Jarvis

Tedronai's personal scribe. Neutral Good, INT 13. Manages records and correspondence for Tedronai's operations. Reliable and capable — more than a note-taker.

Asset

Tedronai's Galleon

A galleon owned by Tedronai. Details on crew, name, and home port to be added. Represents significant logistical capability for the guild.

NPC

Tedronai

Wild Mage and guild employer. Operates from Freeport, managing both the guild and various holdings in the region. Intelligent, measured, and less helpless in a fight than he appears. His true nature as a Wild Mage is not widely known.
