Location

Freeport

A port city currently under siege. Home to the guild and the seat of Lord Klaude's authority. Population and political details to be added.

Location

The Black Crystal Pyramid

A volcanic structure of obsidian-like crystal. Purpose and origin unknown. Contains at least one piece of the relic. Guarded by ash-creatures and pillar-controlling entities.

Item

The Gem of True Teleportation

Provided by Lord Klaude to Tedronai. Three uses, three locations, three relic pieces. Two uses remain.

Item

The Eternal Kindling

Sticks recovered from the pyramid's first level. Flame burns real but the wood does not char. Significance unknown.

NPC

Lord Klaude

Lord of Freeport. Commissioned Tedronai to retrieve a relic capable of ending the siege. Provided the gem of true teleportation.

NPC

Tedronai

Wild Mage. Guild employer. Delegated the relic mission to the party with minimal briefing. Further details to be added.
