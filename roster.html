Guild Member

Stilgar

Human Myrmidon · Level 8 · Neutral Good

6'3", 230 lbs. Black hair, blue eyes. Field commander and record-keeper for Tedronai's operations in Freeport. Longest-serving operative. Reluctant leader by appointment — equally comfortable as just another blade on a team. Manages Tedronai's holdings when not in the field.

Weapons: Two-handed sword (master), longsword (proficient)
Armor: Full plate
Items: Ring of Jumping, Ring of Feather Fall

Proficiencies: Reading/Writing, Rope Use, Land-Based Riding, Iron Will, Endurance, Leadership, Fire Building, Ancient History

Guild Employer

Tedronai

Human Wild Mage · Level 13 · Chaotic Good

6'0", 185 lbs. Brown hair, green eyes. Still young. Employer of the guild and the mind behind its operations in Freeport. Rarely engages in direct combat — prefers to observe a fight from the rear and intervene precisely where needed. His DEX 16 surprises those who expect a bookish wizard, and his INT 21 — boosted over time by carefully acquired librams — surprises everyone else.

A Wild Mage who keeps that fact close to his chest. Avoids casting spells directly for reasons he does not discuss, relying instead on items that consume charges. Few in the party know what he truly is. Fewer still have thought to ask.

Owns a galleon and employs a scribe named Jarvis to manage records and correspondence.

Weapons: Quarterstaff, Dagger of Throwing
Items: Cloak of the Manta Ray, Slippers of Spider Climbing, Belt of Many Pouches, Ring of Regeneration, Ring of Protection

Guild Member

Juniper

Forest druid and wanderer. 5'5", 112 lbs, red hair, green eyes. Worships Ehlonna. Carries a quarterstaff. Languages: Common, Druidic, Sylvan, Elvish, Treant, Goblin.

Guild Associate

Dr. Jill Jensil

Human Alchemist · Biogenesis · Resident Guild Alchemist

5'9", 110 lbs, 27 years old. Red hair, blue-green eyes. The guild encountered Dr. Jensil mid-experiment — the nature of which remains her business. Whatever she was working on left a mark. She now shares her body with Mrs. Hilde, a transformation almost certainly connected to her biogenesis research. Tedronai recognized her value immediately and made his case. She accepted.

Dr. Jill Jensil: STR 10, DEX 9, CON 12, INT 17, WIS 13, CHA 9

Those with the need to know are aware that Dr. Jensil also manifests as Mrs. Hilde — a hulking, barely-coherent alter ego with near-opposite capabilities. What triggers the transformation, how long it lasts, and whether Dr. Jensil retains any awareness during it are questions the guild has learned not to ask too loudly.

Mrs. Hilde: STR 19, DEX 17, CON 18, INT 4, WIS 9, CHA 4

Levels and abilities not formally tracked. The alchemy works. The rest is on a need-to-know basis.

Proficiencies: Alchemy, Anatomy, Glassblowing, Reading/Writing, Brewing, Pottery, Herbalism, Agriculture, Cooking
